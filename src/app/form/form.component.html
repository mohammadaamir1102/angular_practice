        What are Angular Forms?
        Developing forms requires design skill as well as framework support for two-way data binding,
        change tracking, validation, error handling, etc. The Angular Framework, provides two different ways
        to collect and validate the data from a user. They are as follows:

        1-Template-Driven Forms 
        2-Model-Driven Forms (Reactive Forms)
